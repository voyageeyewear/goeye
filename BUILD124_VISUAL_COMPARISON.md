## ğŸ¯ Key Improvements

### 1. BIGGER Images (50% vs 38%)
- **Why:** Products are the star - they should be prominent!
- **Impact:** Customers can see product details much better
- **Result:** More engaging, better conversion

- **Why:** Readability is crucial for e-commerce
- **Impact:** Easier to read on all devices
- **Result:** Better user experience, reduced eye strain

### 3. Even Spacing (SpaceEvenly vs fixed 1px)
- **Why:** Professional apps have consistent spacing
- **Impact:** Visual rhythm, easier to scan
- **Result:** Premium, polished appearance

### 4. More Padding (8px vs 2px)
- **Why:** Breathing room makes content digestible
- **Impact:** Less cluttered, more focused
- **Result:** Modern, clean design

### 5. Bigger Buttons (36px vs 26-28px)
- **Why:** Easier to tap, better mobile UX
- **Impact:** Reduced tap errors, better accessibility
- **Result:** Smoother shopping experience

### 6. No Wasted Space
- **Why:** Every pixel should serve a purpose
- **Impact:** Professional appearance
- **Result:** Tight, polished layout
